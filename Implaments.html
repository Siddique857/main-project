Dental Implants

Implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots, topped with crowns for natural function. They're a permanent alternative to dentures.

Detailed Steps:

  1. Planning:

     CT scans for bone assessment.

  2. Surgery:

     Implant inserted; healing cap placed (osseointegration takes 3–6 months).

  3. Abutment:

     Connector attached.

  4. Crown:

     Custom crown fitted.

  5. Follow-Up:

     Checks for stability.

Benefits:

95% success rate, durable (20+ years), preserves bone.

Risks:

Infection, nerve damage, implant failure; requires healthy bone.

Aftercare:

Antibiotics, soft diet; daily cleaning.

Cost:

₹30,000–₹1 lakh per implant; $1,000–$4,000.

Duration:

3–6 months total, with surgery in 1–2 hours.
